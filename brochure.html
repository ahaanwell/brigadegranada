Brigade Granada Brochure PDF Overview

The Brigade Granada Brochure PDF provides a complete overview of the project, helping buyers make an informed decision before booking. It includes detailed information such as floor plans, pricing structure, amenities, specifications, location advantages, and developer insights—all in a single downloadable document available on this page.

Brigade Granada is a 20-acre pre-launch township project located on Whitefield–Hoskote Road in East Bangalore. The development features 14 high-rise towers with around 2,000 luxury apartments available in 2.5 BHK, 3 BHK, and 4 BHK configurations. The starting price is approximately ₹1.45 Crore, and the official launch is scheduled for 5 December 2026.

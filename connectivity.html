Brigade Granada Connectivity

Brigade Granada Connectivity

Brigade Granada enjoys strong and well-planned connectivity through major road networks, metro access, railway stations, and the international airport. Located along the Whitefield–Hoskote growth corridor, the project benefits from seamless travel options that connect residents to key employment hubs, social infrastructure, and city hotspots.

For homebuyers, connectivity plays a crucial role in daily convenience. Easy access to workplaces, schools, hospitals, and entertainment zones ensures a smooth urban lifestyle with reduced travel stress.

Road Connectivity

The project is well connected through a strong road network that links East Bangalore to other parts of the city. Key roads near Brigade Granada include:

  • NH 75
  • SH 104
  • SH 35
  • NH 648
  • Kadugodi Road
  • Malur Road
  • Kolar Road

These highways and arterial roads improve connectivity to Whitefield, KR Puram, and central Bangalore, making daily commuting more efficient.

Metro Connectivity

The nearest metro access point is:

Namma Metro (Kadugodi Station – Purple Line)

Kadugodi Metro Station is located approximately 3.3 km from the project and can be reached within a short 5–10 minute drive. The metro network significantly reduces travel time to major business districts across Bangalore.

Bus Connectivity

BMTC bus services are available throughout the Whitefield–Hoskote belt, connecting the area to major parts of the city.

Whitefield Bus Stop – approx. 5.9 km

Frequent bus routes ensure affordable and accessible daily travel for residents and office commuters.

Railway Connectivity

The nearest railway station is:

Whitefield Railway Station – approx. 2.8 km

It offers easy access to intercity and intracity train routes, improving long-distance travel convenience for residents.

Airport Connectivity

Kempegowda International Airport – approx. 32 km via SH 104

The airport is reachable within 45–60 minutes depending on traffic conditions, making it convenient for frequent flyers and business travelers.

Connectivity to Major Localities

Brigade Granada is close to several established residential and commercial zones:

These locations are known for IT hubs, shopping centers, and premium residential developments.

Connectivity to IT Hubs

The project is strategically located near major employment centers in East Bangalore:

  • ITPL – 6.1 km
  • EPIP Zone – 9.6 km

This proximity significantly reduces daily commute time for IT professionals.

Conclusion

Brigade Granada offers excellent connectivity across all transport modes—road, metro, rail, and air. Its strategic location in East Bangalore ensures quick access to IT hubs, schools, hospitals, and commercial centers. With improving infrastructure and upcoming transport developments, the connectivity advantage of this project is expected to grow further, making it a strong choice for both end-users and investors.

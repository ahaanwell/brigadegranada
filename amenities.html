Brigade Granada Amenities

Brigade Granada offers more than 35 carefully planned amenities that focus on sports, fitness, wellness, and leisure. These facilities are integrated within a large 80% open space development, supported by a premium 50,000 sq. ft. clubhouse in East Bangalore. All amenities are designed for residents and tenants without any additional usage charges.

The project is currently in its pre-launch stage, and the complete amenities list will be finalized before the official launch scheduled for 5 December 2026. Designed by the Brigade Group, the development aims to support a balanced lifestyle for families, working professionals, and senior citizens.

The community includes dedicated zones for recreation, health, relaxation, and social interaction, ensuring that residents can enjoy a complete lifestyle within the township itself without needing to travel outside for daily needs.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Can tenants use amenities at Brigade Granada or only apartment owners?

All residents, including both homeowners and tenants, can access the 35+ amenities inside Brigade Granada. Entry is restricted to residents only, ensuring privacy, safety, and a less crowded environment within the community.

2. What is the size of the Brigade Granada clubhouse?

The clubhouse at Brigade Granada is approximately 50,000 sq. ft. and is developed across multiple levels including basement, ground, and upper floors. It features around 19 indoor facilities such as a gym, spa, banquet hall, co-working areas, mini theatre, and more, along with 7 rooftop-level amenities like a sunset deck and rooftop gym.

3. What security systems are provided in the project?

Brigade Granada is equipped with modern security infrastructure including boom barriers, CCTV surveillance, video door phones, 24/7 security personnel, and a public announcement system. Each tower has controlled access points to ensure complete safety for residents and visitors.

4. Do apartments facing amenities like the pool have higher pricing?

Yes, units that overlook premium features such as the swimming pool, landscaped gardens, or open courts are priced higher due to better views and higher demand. Buyers can request the latest availability and pricing details for such premium-facing units from the sales team.

5. Are clubhouse and amenities included in the apartment cost?

Yes, all residents get full access to the 35+ amenities without any additional usage charges. Facilities such as the clubhouse, gym, swimming pool, and sports areas are included as part of the community offering. Only premium-facing apartments carry a pricing advantage based on location within the project.

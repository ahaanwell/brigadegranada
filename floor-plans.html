Brigade Granada Floor Plans

Brigade Granada offers thoughtfully designed floor plans for 2.5, 3, and 4 BHK apartments, with super built-up areas ranging from approximately 1,200 sq. ft. to 2,800 sq. ft. The development comprises around 2,000 residential units spread across 14 high-rise towers, each going up to 24 floors, located on Whitefield–Hoskote Road in East Bangalore. To ensure a clutter-free environment, every tower is equipped with three levels of basement parking, allowing the ground level to be dedicated to landscaped spaces and lifestyle amenities.

Detailed floor plans are available in both 2D and 3D formats, helping buyers visualize the layout more clearly. You can also download the Brigade Granada floor plan PDF from this page to review configurations, compare unit sizes, and understand room arrangements, including balcony placements across different apartment types.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What types of floor plans are available at Brigade Granada?

Brigade Granada features 2.5, 3, and 4 BHK apartment configurations. Sizes start from around 1,200 sq. ft. for 2.5 BHK units and go up to 2,800 sq. ft. for larger 4 BHK homes. Each configuration comes with multiple layout options spread across 14 towers rising up to 24 floors.

2. Is it possible to modify the floor plan after booking?

No, modifications to the floor plan are not permitted after booking. The layouts are pre-designed with careful planning to ensure Vaastu compliance, efficient space utilization, and proper ventilation. Buyers can, however, choose their preferred unit, floor level, and facing before finalizing the purchase.

3. Do the apartments receive good natural light and airflow?

Yes, all apartments are designed to maximize natural light and cross ventilation. The towers are strategically spaced to avoid obstruction, ensuring proper airflow and daylight in every unit. Additionally, units are positioned to maintain privacy, with no direct facing between apartments.

4. What is the size range of apartments in the project?

The apartment sizes at Brigade Granada range from 1,200 sq. ft. to 2,800 sq. ft.

  • 2.5 BHK: 1,200 to 1,400 sq. ft.
  • 3 BHK: 1,600 to 1,950 sq. ft.
  • 4 BHK: 2,200 to 2,800 sq. ft.

5. How many apartments are there in Brigade Granada?

The project comprises approximately 2,000 premium apartments distributed across 14 high-rise towers. Each tower includes 24 residential floors along with 3 basement levels dedicated to parking. The development is part of a large township located on Whitefield–Hoskote Road in East Bangalore.

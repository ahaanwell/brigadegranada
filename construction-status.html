Brigade Granada Construction Status

Brigade Granada follows a well-planned construction lifecycle to keep buyers informed at every stage of development. The project progress is divided into key milestones from planning to possession.

Key Construction Milestones

Buyers can track the development through the following stages:

  • Pre-Launch Phase
  • New Launch Phase
  • Current Project Status
  • Construction Progress Updates
  • Expected Completion Date
  • Possession Timeline
  • Latest Site Updates & Photos

Pre-Launch Stage Overview

The pre-launch phase is the foundation stage of the project where planning, design, and approvals take place before official sales begin.

Planning & Architectural Design

During this stage, expert architects design the entire township layout, including the 14 residential towers and master plan.

  • Around 73% efficient space planning ensures optimal apartment layouts
  • Nearly 80% of the project area is dedicated to open green spaces and amenities
  • Structural safety and engineering compliance are finalized before execution

Legal & Government Approvals

The developer ensures all necessary approvals are in place before construction begins.

  • Land ownership verification and legal clearance
  • Government approvals for residential high-rise development
  • RERA registration process underway
  • Compliance with planning authorities like BMRDA and BDA

This ensures complete transparency and secure investment for buyers.

New Launch Stage

  • Official Launch Timeline: December 2025
  • Model apartments become available for visits
  • Early booking options with priority unit selection
  • Attractive pre-launch pricing and payment plans offered
  • Sales assistance provided for unit selection and configuration

Current Project Status

Brigade Granada is currently in the pre-launch to early development stage. Physical construction activities will gradually scale up after groundwork completion.

Initial Construction Activities

  • Site preparation and land leveling
  • Excavation for 3-level basement structure (3B)
  • Foundation work for high-rise towers
  • Use of modern construction technology like Mivan system
  • Continuous quality inspection at every stage

Completion Timeline

The overall project completion is expected around 2030, depending on construction phases and regulatory timelines.

Possession Timeline

Home possession is expected to begin from 2030 onwards, after full project completion.

At the time of handover, buyers will receive:

  • Occupancy Certificate (OC)
  • Completion Certificate (CC)
  • Registered Sale & Possession Documents

  • Parking and infrastructure development

Buyer Development Updates

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the current status of Brigade Granada?

The project is currently in the pre-launch phase, where planning, approvals, and initial site preparation activities are underway.

2. When will the construction start?

The main construction work, including tower development, is expected to begin in early 2026 after completion of initial groundwork.

3. What is the total land area of the project?

Brigade Granada is spread across approximately 20.19 acres, with nearly 80% dedicated to open spaces, greenery, and amenities.

4. Are quality checks conducted during construction?

Yes, Brigade Group follows a strict quality control process, with inspections carried out at every stage from foundation to final finishing.

5. What documents are provided at the time of possession?

At handover, buyers will receive all essential legal documents, including:

  • Occupation Certificate (OC)
  • Completion Certificate (CC)
  • Possession Letter

6. What is the expected completion timeline?

The project is planned for full completion around 2030, subject to construction phases and approvals.

7. How can buyers track project progress?

Buyers can stay updated through official Brigade Granada construction updates, newsletters, site visits, or by contacting the project sales office.

Brigade Granada Payment Plan

Brigade Granada offers a construction-linked payment plan, allowing buyers to pay in stages as the project progresses. An initial amount of 10% is required at the time of booking to secure the unit. The remaining balance is paid in structured instalments aligned with key construction milestones. Additional charges such as stamp duty, registration fees, and GST are not included in the base payment plan and must be paid separately during the registration process.

Brigade Granada Payment Schedule

The payment structure is designed to ensure flexibility and transparency for buyers. The instalment breakdown is as follows:

  • At the time of booking: 10% of the total cost
  • At the time of agreement signing: 20% of the total cost
  • On commencement of foundation work: 10%
  • On completion of ground floor slab: 5%
  • On completion of 2nd floor slab: 5%
  • On completion of 4th floor slab: 5%
  • On completion of 6th floor slab: 5%
  • On completion of 8th floor slab: 5%
  • On completion of 10th floor slab: 5%
  • On completion of 12th floor slab: 5%
  • On completion of 14th floor slab: 5%
  • On completion of 16th floor slab: 5%
  • On completion of 18th floor slab: 5%
  • On completion of flooring work: 5%
  • At the time of possession/hand over: 5%

This milestone-based plan helps buyers manage their finances more effectively while ensuring payments are directly linked to the progress of construction.

Brigade Group Overview

Brigade Group is one of India’s most established real estate developers, with a strong legacy that began in 1986 in Bangalore. Founded by M.R. Jaishankar, the company has grown steadily over nearly four decades, shaping residential, commercial, retail, and hospitality developments across India.

Today, Brigade Group operates in multiple major cities including Chennai, Hyderabad, Kochi, Mysuru, Mangaluru, Ahmedabad, and other fast-growing urban markets.

With a track record of 280+ completed projects and over 86 million sq ft of developed space, the company has become a trusted name among homebuyers seeking quality construction and long-term value.

Leadership of Brigade Group

The company is led by its founder M.R. Jaishankar, who serves as the Executive Chairman. Under his leadership, Brigade evolved from a single-city developer into a diversified real estate brand with national presence.

The next generation of leadership is actively involved in the company’s growth:

Pavitra Shankar – Managing Director

Nirupa Shankar – Joint Managing Director

Their leadership brings a mix of strategic expansion and modern development practices, strengthening the brand’s position in India’s competitive real estate sector.

Key Milestones in Brigade Group’s Growth

Brigade Group has steadily expanded its footprint through major milestones:

1986 – Company founded in Bangalore

1990s – Expansion into commercial real estate

2004 – Launch of mixed-use development projects

2010 – Entry into hospitality with premium hotel developments

2013 – Listed on NSE India and BSE India

These milestones reflect the company’s evolution into a diversified real estate powerhouse.

Commercial & Mixed-Use Development

Brigade Group also has a strong presence in commercial real estate. One of its early landmark developments was Brigade Towers on Brigade Road, Bangalore, which marked its entry into large-scale commercial projects.

Over time, the company expanded into retail spaces, office complexes, and integrated townships, strengthening its diversified portfolio.

Financial Strength & Performance

Brigade Group has demonstrated strong financial performance with:

Revenue of approximately ₹2,384 Crore (FY25)

Net worth of around ₹25,900 Crore (2026 estimate)

280+ delivered projects across India

This financial stability reflects consistent demand, timely project delivery, and strong brand trust in the real estate market.

Future Growth of Brigade Group

Brigade Group is now expanding into Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities, focusing on sustainable development and green building practices. The company is also strengthening its presence in integrated townships and large-scale residential communities.

With infrastructure growth across India and rising housing demand, Brigade Group is expected to continue its expansion while maintaining its reputation for quality construction.

Conclusion

Brigade Group has built a strong legacy of trust, innovation, and consistent delivery since 1986. Its presence across major Indian cities and diversified portfolio makes it one of the most reliable real estate developers in the country.
